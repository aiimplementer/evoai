// How we work

Simple. Fast. Effective.

No endless discovery phases, no bloated timelines. Just clear steps from first conversation to live automation deployment — and then scale.

01
💬 Discover & Define
Week 1–2
We start with a focused conversation to understand your business, challenges, and goals. Map every repetitive, rule-based, or data-heavy workflow. Score each by effort-vs-impact. Ignore the rest.
  • Workshop with ops leads to surface top pain points
  • Score using Impact × Feasibility × Data Readiness matrix
  • Lock in top 3 automation candidates for piloting
  • Honest discussion about where AI can make the biggest impact
02
⚙️ Build & Integrate
Week 2–5
We design and build a tailored AI solution that fits your existing stack. Fast iterations, full transparency, and you stay involved at every step. Document the exact workflow, define inputs, outputs, decision points, and exceptions.
  • Map full process — identify data sources, APIs and integration points
  • Build MVP using LLM + workflow automation tools
  • Run parallel test (AI vs. human) for 1 week with real data
  • Iterate on edge cases and exception handling
03
🚀 Deploy & Scale
Week 5+
We launch, monitor, and refine. Go live with guardrails — set up alerting, human-in-the-loop escalation, and a dashboard so you always know what the system is doing. Once it's working, we help you scale across your organisation.
  • Deploy to production with rollback plan and monitoring alerts
  • Weekly review cadence for the first 30 days
  • Document playbook for internal reuse and team onboarding
  • Identify next 5 automation candidates and stay on hand as your AI ambitions grow

// Automation principles

The Golden Rules

Hard-won lessons from deploying AI automation across dozens of organisations. Follow these — or pay the price later.

✓ Always Do
  • Start with one process. Prove ROI. Then expand.
  • Involve the team who owns the process from day one.
  • Build human-in-the-loop escalation paths for every automation.
  • Log every AI decision with a timestamp and reason.
  • Set measurable success criteria before building anything.
  • Test with real, messy production data — not clean samples.
✕ Never Do
  • Automate a broken process — fix it first, then automate it.
  • Deploy without monitoring, alerts, or a rollback plan.
  • Build bespoke when a no-code tool covers 80% of the need.
  • Send AI-generated output to customers without a human review step initially.
  • Optimise for cost before you've validated the automation works.
  • Ignore edge cases — they become production incidents at 2am.
