Pro Tips
🗂️
Server-side pagination: Only 15 leads are fetched per page. Use "Last" to jump to the final page, or type a page number in the "Go to" box.
⏰
Overdue filter: Click "Overdue Follow-ups" in the sidebar to instantly see all leads with past follow-up dates (scans up to 500 records).
💰
Pipeline Value: Computed once on load from the first 500 active leads (excludes Won/Lost). Hover the stat card for details.
🎯
Maximise scores fast: Fill in phone, LinkedIn, and deal value — these three fields alone add up to +45 points.
🔄
Board view for pipeline: Drag cards between columns to instantly update a lead's stage. Each column loads up to 200 leads.
🔍
Search scope: Search works on Table & List views only. Board view always shows the full pipeline regardless of search.
📤
Export All: Use with caution — it reads every lead document from Firestore and may affect your billing quota on large datasets.
