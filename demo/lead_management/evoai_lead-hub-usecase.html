One hub. Two actions. Full pipeline.

The evoAI Lead Hub combines an intelligent intake form with a real-time lead viewer — connected end-to-end by n8n automation and Firebase.

// How It Works

From enquiry to insight in 4 steps

01

Visitor submits form

They enter their details in the Lead Hub's Add Lead tab. Client-side validation catches errors instantly.

02

Webhook fires to n8n

A POST request carries the structured JSON payload to the n8n workflow — no delays, no middleware.

03

Firebase stores the lead

n8n writes the record to Firebase Realtime Database with a timestamp. Fully persistent and queryable.

04

Team views & acts

Any authorised user clicks "Load Leads" in the View Leads tab to see the full, sorted pipeline instantly.
